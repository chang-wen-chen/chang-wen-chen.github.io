English Synonyms

appraise | evaluation

What are the differences

Posted by Eirik on 22 Feb, 2024

“Evaluate” and “appraise” are two terms that involve assessing something, but they have slightly different connotations and applications.

Evaluate: This term implies a comprehensive assessment or analysis of something, often with the aim of understanding its worth, quality, effectiveness, or significance. Evaluation typically involves considering various factors, gathering information, and making judgments or conclusions based on criteria. It’s a broader term that can be applied in various contexts, such as academic research, performance reviews, or product testing.

Appraise: On the other hand, “appraise” typically refers to the act of determining the monetary value or worth of something, especially in the context of assessing the value of property, goods, or assets. It often involves examining the item in question, considering its condition, rarity, demand, and other relevant factors, and arriving at an estimate of its value. Appraisals are commonly conducted in real estate transactions, insurance claims, and antique evaluations.

繁體中文：

「評估」和「評價」是兩個涉及評估事物的詞語，但它們在含義和應用上略有不同。

評估 (Evaluate)：這個詞意味著對某事物進行全面評估或分析，通常旨在了解其價值、質量、效能或重要性。評估通常涉及考慮各種因素、收集信息，並根據標準進行判斷或得出結論。這是一個更廣泛的術語，可應用於各種情境，例如學術研究、績效評估或產品測試。

評價 (Appraise)：另一方面，「評價」通常指的是確定某事物的價值或價值，特別是在評估財產、商品或資產的價值時。它通常涉及檢查所討論的物品，考慮其狀況、稀有性、需求和其他相關因素，並估計其價值。評價通常在房地產交易、保險索賠和古董估價等方面進行。

例句： Evaluate:

English: The teacher will evaluate the students’ performance based on their understanding of the material and their ability to apply it in real-life situations. Chinese: 老師將根據學生對材料的理解程度以及他們在現實情況中應用材料的能力來評估他們的表現。 Appraise:

English: Before selling the antique vase, it’s important to have it appraised to determine its true market value. Chinese: 在出售古董花瓶之前，重要的是將其估價，以確定其真正的市場價值。
