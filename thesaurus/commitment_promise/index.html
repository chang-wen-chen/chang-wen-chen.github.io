English Synonyms

commitment | promise

What are the differences

Posted by Eirik on 1 Feb, 2024

TLDR

A commitment holds more weight than a simple promise

They do differ in how they frame the distinction:

Emphasis on intent:

  • Commitment signifies a genuine desire to fulfill the promise, suggesting sincerity and motivation.

Focus on strength:

  • Commitment implies a stronger promise than one can readily deliver, highlighting its ambitious nature.

Contrast with indulgence:

  • Commitment stands against indulgence, emphasizing dedication and perseverance as key differentiators.

In essence, commitments go beyond empty promises, demonstrating a willingness to put in the effort and potentially exceed expectations.

“Commitment” typically implies a dedication or obligation to a certain course of action or relationship. It often involves a sense of duty or responsibility.

“Promise” generally refers to a declaration or assurance that one will do something or that a particular outcome will occur. It’s a pledge or guarantee of action or result.

“承諾” 通常意味著對某種行動或關係的奉獻或義務。它通常涉及到一種責任或義務感。

“諾言” 一般是指對某件事情或某種結果的聲明或保證。它是對行動或結果的承諾或保證。
